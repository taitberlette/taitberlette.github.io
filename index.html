When I became a teenager, I started to drift away from the mechanical side of things and got more into computer programming. For a while I actually thought I was going to pursue computer science as a degree, which I think is influenced from my dad, who works with computers a lot! The feeling of having something I built in my hands was far greater than anything I ever got programming, where it's just some code in magic computer land, nothing real...
